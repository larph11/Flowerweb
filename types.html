Types of Flowers

Rose

Scientific Name: Rosa

Roses are one of the most popular and widely cultivated flowers, known for their beauty and variety of colors.

Tulip

Scientific Name: Tulipa

Tulips are spring-blooming flowers that come in a wide range of colors and are native to Eurasia.

Daisy

Scientific Name: Bellis perennis

Daisies are small, simple flowers with a yellow center and white petals. They are often associated with innocence and purity.

Orchid

Scientific Name: Orchidaceae

Orchids are known for their diverse and stunning flowers, which come in a wide variety of shapes, sizes, and colors.

Sunflower

Scientific Name: Helianthus annuus

Sunflowers are large, bright flowers with yellow petals and a dark center. They are known for their association with sunshine and happiness.
